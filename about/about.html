About

Concept

The main purpose of ASA Stats is to evaluate and present the current balance in Algos for any address on the Algorand blockchain. Such a balance is calculated by adding the existing ALGO balance and the estimated sum of values of all the ASAs account holds.

The final phase of development for the inclusion of accounts' NFT values is expected to take place after the Decipher event. That would probably coincide with the finished ASA Stats Token presale phase.

In this first phase, users have to refresh an address page in order to receive the latest data. Real-time account tracking development is expected to take place either during or after the implementation of the NFT evaluation functionality.

An automatic throttling/rate-limiting development will take place afterward, as one of the requirements for both business use and adoption of ASASTATS utility token.

Goals

The primary goal is to provide a great user experience. If that can be achieved, then establishment of project's DAO would become the very next primary goal.

ASA Stats aims to have price information on every ASA and NFT that has ever been sold.

For those that have never been sold, the price evaluation algorithms would be developed and if possible an informative price will be presented to the user together with the reasoning for such evaluation.

ASA Stats will implement every publicly available SDK (preferably Python) of all major DEX solutions on the Algorand blockchain as soon as such SDK is available. Also, ASA Stats author (and ideally the team too) will actively contribute to those SDK codebases during the pre-DAO ASA Stats phase. As of February 2022, Tinyman AMM, respectively related py-tinyman-sdk represents the only available SDK for any AMM/DEX on Algorand Mainnet.

It is planned that the next section name changes from "Author", over "Team", to final "DAO".

If you wish to be involved, please subscribe to the ASA Stats subreddit, send a direct message to @asastatscom on Twitter, or check out #tasks channel in our Discord server.
